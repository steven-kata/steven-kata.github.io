Jelly Toss Project
Toss a Jelly to the right to collide with the monsters.
Project Details / Background
2D physics based game where you toss a jelly into obstacles to get rid of monsters. You can fling the bear by dragging it to the left and aiming towards the right. when you are ready to toss, let go of the mouse or touchscreen to see it fly. Keep trying to rid the world of the cute monsters to beat the level.
Please visit this project's Itch IO page to see more details or to try the game out.
